This project is a research tool for analyzing Riot Games' Vanguard anti-cheat system. It documents Vanguard's kernel driver behavior, detection mechanisms, and system-level protections through reverse engineering analysis. The tool may include utilities for monitoring Vanguard's driver activity, enumerating its callbacks, or logging its kernel operations. It is aimed at anti-cheat researchers studying Vanguard's kernel-level protection architecture and detection methodology.
